To the one who deserves gentleness,

I know I overreacted, and I honestly shouldn’t have acted that way. I let my emotions get the better of me and ended up saying things that probably hurt you, and I’m really sorry for that.

You didn’t deserve it at all. And what honestly made me realize my mistake even more was how understanding and patient you were through everything. Most people would’ve reacted differently, but you still handled it with so much kindness.

That’s something I really admire about you. You have such a caring heart, and the way you stay calm and understanding even in difficult moments says a lot about the kind of person you are.

I never want to be someone who makes you feel upset when you deserve so much better than that. Thank you for being patient with me, and thank you for being the amazing person you are. 💜
